    Domisili

    Bungo is a district in Jambi Province which has natural beauty and rich culture. Bungo Regency has its capital in Muara Bungo, which is the center of the regional economy and government. This region has various potentials, including the plantation, agriculture and trade sectors. Bungo is also known for the cultural diversity and traditions of its people, most of whom come from the Jambi Malay tribe. Apart from that, this area has various natural tourist destinations, such as Tegan Kiri Waterfall, Lake Forty, and Cinto Kenang Hill. Infrastructure in Bungo continues to develop, with main roads connecting to major cities in Sumatra, as well as increasingly better education and health facilities.
